1. Introduction

At Cymbal Insurance, we believe that privacy is the ultimate luxury. Our Digital Concierge service is built on a foundation of absolute transparency and unyielding security. This policy outlines how we handle your personal information with the same care and precision we bring to our bespoke insurance solutions.

By using the Cymbal Sovereign platform, you entrust us with sensitive details. We honor that trust by implementing industry-leading data governance protocols designed to protect your identity and your legacy.

2. Information We Collect

person

Direct Identifiers

Name, address, contact information, and government-issued identification required for policy underwriting and verification.

analytics

Behavioral Data

Interaction logs with the Cymbal Sovereign platform, including claim filing frequency and feature usage to improve concierge services.

3. Data Usage Purpose

Your data is never "productized." We utilize information exclusively to enhance your insurance experience and provide proactive risk management.

  • check_circle Personalized policy refinement based on real-time asset valuation.
  • check_circle Expedited claim processing through automated AI analysis of submitted documentation.
  • check_circle Fraud prevention and anti-money laundering compliance checks.

4. Security Infrastructure

We employ AES-256 bit encryption for all data at rest and TLS 1.3 for all data in transit. Our security operations center (SOC) monitors the Sovereign infrastructure 24/7/365 to identify and mitigate emerging threats before they can impact our clients.

Furthermore, we implement strict physical security at our data centers, ensuring that your digital footprint is protected by multiple layers of physical and logical barriers.

5. Your Global Privacy Rights

Regardless of your jurisdiction, Cymbal provides the right to access, rectify, or erase your personal data. You may also object to processing or request data portability at any time through your Concierge dashboard.
